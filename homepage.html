About FWM:

FWM is not just a website; it's a movement led by students like you to revolutionize food waste management. Join us in the mission to reduce waste, optimize meals, and make your dining experience more sustainable!

Our Key Features:

Student-Driven Feedback System: Empower yourself! Our unique system relies on your valuable feedback to drive waste reduction initiatives. Your voice shapes the future of dining.
Witness the impact! Track how your feedback contributes to waste reduction, creating an accountable food management system.
Customized Meal Plans: Your preferences matter. Our system adapts to your feedback, crafting meal plans that align with your tastes and values.
